# Tag Rollups

Tag rollups show which themes appear across calls without forcing managers to read every transcript.

## Suggested Tags

- `security`: buyer asked about data handling, trust, or review scope.
- `procurement`: commercial approval or vendor review is active.
- `budget`: timing or funding is part of the objection.
- `pilot`: buyer needs proof from a limited rollout.
- `expansion`: a successful team is pulling in another team.

## Coaching Use

1. Sort tags by call count.
2. Pick the highest-frequency tag that also appears in open pipeline.
3. Review one strong call and one weak call for that tag.
4. Turn the comparison into one follow-up writing habit.

## Interpreting Reports

- A high-count tag is not automatically a problem.
- A tag that appears across many owners may indicate a team coaching topic.
- A tag that appears for one account may indicate a deal-specific blocker.
- Managers should compare tags to outcomes before choosing a coaching focus.
- Repeated tags should be checked against the next week's replay set.
