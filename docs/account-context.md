# Account Context

Account context keeps replay feedback grounded in the buyer's situation.

## Review Uses

- Segment helps managers compare similar buying motions.
- Industry language keeps examples concrete during coaching.
- Buying motion explains why the next step matters.
- Review focus gives the manager a short checklist for the call.

## Example Manager Notes

- Northstar Health: validate security evidence before expansion.
- Harbor Ledger: connect pilot evidence to procurement readiness.
- BrightCart: tie the reconnect plan to budget planning.
- Cedar Support: show escalation reduction in the technical readout.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Every sample account has an industry and segment.
- Every buying motion is specific enough for a manager note.
- Every review focus can be mapped to a transcript excerpt.
